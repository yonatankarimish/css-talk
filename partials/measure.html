Units of Measure

Unit Meaning
px 1/96 of an inch. Absolute value.
% Of the parent element. Relative value.
vh 1% of the screen height. Relative value.
vw 1% of the screen width. Relative value.
em The font size of the current element.
vmin 1% of the viewport's smaller dimension.
vmax 1% of the viewport's larger dimension.
